Who We Are

Weavers of Heritage

Kashi Rivaz is Varanasi's trusted destination for handwoven Banarasi sarees. For over a decade, we have walked alongside master weavers in the narrow lanes of Banaras — preserving techniques that trace back to the Mughal era.

Every saree that leaves our looms carries the fingerprint of an artisan — the invisible signature of someone who has devoted their life to an art form older than most nations.

"A Banarasi saree is not cloth — it is poetry in silk."
Our Mission

Craft Preserved, Culture Shared

Our mission is simple: bring authentic Banarasi weaves to every home — across India and beyond. We believe that luxury should be honest, and tradition should be accessible.

We work directly with weavers, cutting out middlemen to ensure fair wages, dignified working conditions, and a sustainable livelihood for every artisan family.
